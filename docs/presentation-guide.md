### Slide 2: Problem Context
- **The Challenge**: Misinformation is traditionally treated as binary (True vs. False). However, in high-volatility environments, the *risk of harm* is the critical metric.
- **Where it Exists**: Within the complex Indian socio-political and medical landscape, specifically on encrypted or high-velocity platforms like WhatsApp, Telegram, and X.

### Slide 3: Why This Problem Matters
- **Affected**: Vulnerable communities, public health systems, and regional security.
- **Consequences**: If left unsolved, "fact-checking" comes too late. Misinformation leads to mob mobilization, refusal of life-saving medical care (e.g., vaccine hesitancy), and civil unrest.

### Slide 4: The User
- **Who**: Intelligence Officers and Cyber Cell Analysts.
- **Daily Experience**: Overwhelmed by "viral" noise. They need to distinguish between harmless emotional venting and tactical mobilization messages disguised in regional slang.

### Slide 5: Key Insight
- **The Insight**: Volatility and Intent are more predictive of real-world harm than simple Veracity. A message doesn't have to be "false" to be "lethal" if it incites immediate violence.

### Slide 6: 3C Journey
- **Collecting Dreams**: Visualizing a digital landscape where the weaponization of narratives is neutralized before it escalates to the streets.
- **Creating Solutions**: Developed an AI-driven "Harm Index" that maps linguistic volatility to geospatial hotspots.
- **Changing Lives**: Enabling security forces and health departments to intervene precisely, preventing violence and medical negligence.

### Slide 7: The Solution
- **What**: CrisisGuard—a tactical dashboard that analyzes social media intake using a calibrated 40/40/20 weighted logic.
- **The Logic**: Harm Score = (Emotional Intensity × 0.4) + (Call to Action × 0.4) + (Context Mismatch × 0.2). This prioritizes behavioral intent and mobilization triggers, with scores above 70 automating priority alerts.

### Slide 8: AI-Enabled Prototype
- **Genkit & Gemini**: Leverages Generative AI to provide "Causal Reasoning"—explaining *why* a post is dangerous—and detects "Dog-Whistles" (coded regional slang) that standard keyword filters miss.
- **Multimodal**: Compares text to image/video evidence to identify "Context Mismatches" (disinformation).

### Slide 9: Impact
- **Who Benefits**: Regional security agencies and public health departments.
- **What Improves**: Response time is slashed, resource allocation is data-driven, and risk assessment becomes objective rather than subjective.

### Slide 10: Next Steps
- **Expansion**: Implementing autonomous 24/7 global sector scanning and integrating with emergency dispatch systems (112/100) for closed-loop threat neutralization.
